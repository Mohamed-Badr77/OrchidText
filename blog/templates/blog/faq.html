General Info.

Orchid Texts is free website for everyone. It's purpose was a specific person, but it shall serve as a haven for all that wish to say something to someone.

Feel free to create an account for your own Orchid Texts

Respect and good behavior are a must for Orchid Texts. It was not meant for hate but for love. All posts are moderated.

The website is still a work in progress, and it was made for the sake of one person. Any further changes will be decided later.

About our texts

Orchid Texts are your love and desire put into words. Try writing an orchid text and making your partner guess which one is yours!

All posts are moderated, and no texts with hateful or inappropriate nuances will be tolerated.

All you have to do to create your own Orchid Text is create an account. Once you do that, you would be able to create as many texts as you want.
